Inter School Basketball Compitition
we won first prize in the event.

At the start of the game, the referee tossed the ball at the center of the court between two players. One player from our team try to get their hands on the ball and the ball is passed on to the teammates of our team. For scoring a point, our team needs to shoot the ball through the basket. Then a shot is scored from a distance that is closer to the basket than the 3 point line than it fetches 2 points. Also, if the ball is shot from the distance behind 3 point line, it fetches 3 points. So, the team that has a maximum number of points was declared the winner.
